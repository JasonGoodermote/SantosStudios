Who We Are

Born from a Love
of Beauty & Craft

Carolina Santos Studios was born from a lifelong passion for beauty and the belief that a salon visit should feel like so much more than just an appointment. It should feel like coming home to yourself.

Founded by Carolina Santos — a master stylist and beauty educator with over a decade of experience — our studio was designed as a refuge from the ordinary. Every detail, from the warm scent in the air to the carefully curated service menu, was chosen with intention.

Today, Carolina Santos Studios is home to a team of passionate artists who share one mission: to make every guest feel extraordinary. We don't just do hair, nails, or skin — we create experiences that stay with you long after you've left our chair.
